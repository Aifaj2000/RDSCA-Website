About College

Welcome to Raje Dharmarao College of Science

Dharmarao Shikshan Mandal. Aheri is foundation society which runs this college.

Dharmarao Shikshan Mandal was established by Shrimant Raje Vishveshwar Rao in 1958 at Aheri. Now the society runs more than 50 institutions including senior colleges, Junior colleges. secondary school, primary schools and Hostels (Girls and Boys) etc.

Dharmarao Shikshan Mandal has started Raje Dharmarao College of Science at Aheri, Dist Gadchiroli, Since Session 1987-88 with the Enrolment of the student in B.Sc. part 1 (Math and Biology) Group.

The College meets in two shifts Morning Shift 7 am to 12 pm and Noon Shift From 10.30 am to 5.45 pm. The College shifted to new building since session 2021 -22.

The college hast two storied building situated near Dansur Chouk Aheri, Building includes Four science Laboratories, Five Class room, One Principal Room, Administrative block, Library, Girls common room, Staff room, Office Block, NSS, Sport, Auditorium.

The college is successfully completed 35 years of its service to the cause of Education and Achieved a Sound Academic Progress. NAAC peer team visited the college during 8 & 9 September 2004 to make and assessment of college and have accredited at C++ level with Score 64.60.
