Vission

The vision of institution is to provide higher education to the rural students at low and affordable cost with the quality parameters of national education program and to inculcate committedness and social responsibilities, sense of justice, leadership qualities. and ethical values.

Mission

• To provide quality education to the students to develop their moral values, devotion to duty, spirit of social service.

• To create intellectually stimulating environment for students to acquire skill based knowledge and platform for creativity, innovations to progress towards excellence and to make them vibrant and conversant with current knowledge.

• Spreading science education among the poor needy students, So that they are capable to uplift their life style.

• To inculcate sense of discipline and responsibility towards nature and society.
