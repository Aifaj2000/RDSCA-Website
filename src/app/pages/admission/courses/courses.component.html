The college offers Three year / SIX SEMESTER B.Sc. degree course under CBCS (Choice Based Credit System)
in Science Stream with following subject combinations.


•B.Sc. (PCM)
•B.Sc. (CBZ)
B.Sc. (PCM)

              Students who passed XII science with Mathematics , Physics and Chemistry could join in this stream. In the undergraduate course, English is the first language which is to be studied during First year (Two semesters), and the students could opt either Marathi or Hindi as their second language. Chemistry, Physics & Mathematics are taught for all the six semesters.

B.Sc. (CBZ)

              Students who passed XII science with Biology , Physics and Chemistry could join in this stream. In the undergraduate course, English is the first language which is to be studied during First year (Two semesters), and the students could opt either Marathi or Hindi as their second language. Chemistry, Botany & Zoology are taught for all the six semesters.
